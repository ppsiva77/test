Hydrogen Gas Sensing Detectors

Due to its small molecular size and fast dispersion, hydrogen can leak easily. Even minor leaks pose risks, especially in confined spaces where hydrogen can accumulate and create flammable or suffocating environments. Hydrogen is undetectable by human senses; advanced technology is needed to detect leaks. This is where Eden H2’s Hydrogen Gas Sensing Detectors come into play.

Our Technology Behind Hydrogen Detection

Eden H2's Hydrogen Gas Sensing Detectors use advanced ultrasonic hydrogen sensors for rapid and accurate leak detection. These sensors measure the difference in sound velocity between hydrogen and air. When hydrogen mixes with air, it alters the speed of sound, enabling the detection of even small changes in hydrogen concentration.
