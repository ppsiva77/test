Micro Gas Turbines: A Decentralised Energy Solution

"We deliver environmentally friendly, robust, and resilient power solutions for both domestic and industrial sectors through our advanced micro gas turbine technology."

EDENH2 Micro Gas Turbines (MGTs) offer a flexible, low-emission, and reliable turn-key power solution for India's diverse energy needs. Our turbines are designed to harness the power of hydrogen, significantly reducing carbon emissions and environmental impact.

Key Features:

Fuel Flexibility

Our MGTs can provide peak performance for a variety of liquid and gaseous fuels through proprietary fuel-handling technologies. Compatible fuels include diesel, natural gas, hydrogen, e-fuels, and others.

Proprietary Combustor Technology

Our advanced combustor design ensures optimal performance, high efficiency, and ultra-low emissions for a range of fuels.

Advanced Combined Heat and Power (CHP)

Our systems, with CHP option, can efficiently generate both electricity and heat, maximizing energy efficiency and reducing operational costs.

Secure Operation and Communication

Our technology is designed with robust security features and reliable communication protocols to ensure uninterrupted operation.

Grid Independence

Our systems offer ease of system integration, making them ideal for off-grid applications, offering reliable power supply to remote areas.

Affordable Technology Options

We offer a range of MGT solutions to meet various budget and energy requirements, providing flexible and cost-effective solutions.

Applications:

Rural Electrification

Provision of reliable power supply to homes, schools, and small businesses in remote areas, improving quality of life and economic opportunities.

Public Transport Electrification

Support for charging infrastructure for electric buses and vehicles, reducing air pollution and promoting sustainable transportation.

Integration with Solar Grids

Seamless integration with solar power systems, providing backup power and ensuring continuous energy supply.

Industrial Decarbonisation

Large industries can significantly reduce their carbon footprint by adopting our hydrogen-powered solutions, aligning with India's net-zero emissions goals.
