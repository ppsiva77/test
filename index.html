Company Overview:

Eden H2 is a socially responsible company providing innovative technological solutions for integrating renewable energy with disruptive hydrogen technologies for creating environmentally safe energy storage solutions and promoting the use of clean energy in various domestic and industrial sectors.
The company designs, develops, manufactures, and installs low-carbon, low-cost hydrogen technologies for efficient energy solutions. The company also provides integration solutions to cater Power-to-X and X-to-Power requirements at domestic and industrial scale.
We offer our EPC services predominantly in thrust areas such as Smart Cities, Utilities & Energy Sector, Sensor Integration, and Safety.
